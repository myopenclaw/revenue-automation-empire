## üöÄ STEP-BY-STEP SETUP

### **Step 1: Create ProtonMail Plus Account**
1. Go to **https://proton.me/mail**
2. Click **"Get Proton Mail"**
3. Select **"Plus"** plan (‚Ç¨3.99/month)
4. Enter payment details (credit card)
5. Create main account: `yourname@pm.me`

**Time:** 10 minutes
**Cost:** ‚Ç¨3.99 (first month)

### **Step 2: Setup Email Aliases**
1. Login to ProtonMail
2. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Identity and addresses**
3. Click **"Add address"**
4. Create each alias (10 total)
5. Use format: `[channel-name]@pm.me`

**Time:** 15 minutes
**Note:** Aliases are included in Plus plan

### **Step 3: Configure Email Clients**
**For each alias:**
1. **IMAP/SMTP Settings:**
   - IMAP: imap.protonmail.ch (Port 993, SSL)
   - SMTP: smtp.protonmail.ch (Port 587, STARTTLS)
2. **Username:** Full alias email
3. **Password:** Your main account password

**Time:** 20 minutes (optional, for email clients)

### **Step 4: Test All Aliases**
1. Send test email from each alias
2. Check delivery and replies
3. Verify encryption works
4. Save credentials securely

**Time:** 10 minutes
